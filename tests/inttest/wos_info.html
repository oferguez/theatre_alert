National Youth Dance CompanyBoy Blue NYDC 24/25

Sadler's Wells Theatre, Inner London

About the Show

Founded in 2012, and hosted at Sadler’s Wells, NYDC is an exciting young company that creates and performs innovative and influential dance, drawing together some of the brightest young talent from across the country. The company has a track record of putting young people on paths to successful dance careers, with over 80% of NYDC dancers going onto further dance studies, in vocational training or working professionally.Each year, National Youth Dance Company (NYDC), England’s flagship company for young dancers run by Sadler’s Wells, brings together some of the most talented young dancers from across the country to create, perform and tour a new work by a renowned choreographer who leads the company as Guest Artistic Director. In the past 10 years, over 360 young dancers have passed through the company. For 2025, the company will be led by Sky Arts and Olivier Award-winning hip hop dance theatre company Boy Blue: Kenrick ‘H20’ Sandy and Michael ‘Mikey J’ Asante.
